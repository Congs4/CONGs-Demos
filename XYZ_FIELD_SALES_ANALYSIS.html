Field Sales Outperformance

Field sales revenue (a portion of total dealer sales attributed to salespeople) grew +24% while overall dealer sales grew +1.4% and units dropped -16.9%. Important Note: Current field sales figures represent retrospective attribution of dealer sales to salespeople, not necessarily new business directly generated by the field sales channel. To accurately measure field sales effectiveness, we recommend implementing a tagging system to track sales that are genuinely originated by salespeople (new clients, new business) versus existing dealer customer sales that are subsequently credited to field reps.
